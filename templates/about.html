Divyansh Sharma is an Indian Director born in Jammu & Kashmir, working across scripted film and music videos, at the age of 23 Divyansh wrote, co-produced and directed his first 20 minute film, "I shoot "em down" features Rachit Singh (recently worked in Hotstar+Disney Karma Calling).

His journey continued through his selection at European Film College, Denmark, where he expanded his skills & cinematic language. After graduating, he embarked on the path of independent filmmaking. His latest work includes The Lurking written & directed, co-produced by Kalpa Chitra Productions has been selected at the Wench Film Festival. He’s directed several experimental & gritty short films. Currently working as a Production Coordinator at redchillies color

His motto ‘Get busy living - or get busy dying’ - Ellis ‘Red’
